## How It Works

### Step 1: Student Registration
1. Student visits your website
2. Fills out registration form (name, email, phone)
3. Uploads payment receipt
4. Submits enrollment request

### Step 2: Admin Approval
1. You receive notification of new enrollment
2. Review student information and payment receipt
3. Approve or decline the enrollment
4. Student is automatically notified

### Step 3: Student Access
1. Approved student logs in
2. Views all enrolled courses
3. Watches video lessons
4. Downloads course materials
5. Tracks learning progress
