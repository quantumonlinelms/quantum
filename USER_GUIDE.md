# Quantum LMS - Student User Guide

Welcome to Quantum LMS! This guide will help you navigate the platform and make the most of your learning experience.

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Registration

**Step 1: Create Your Account**
- Visit the registration page
- Fill in your information:
  - Full Name
  - Email Address
  - Phone Number
  - Password (choose a strong password)

**Step 2: Upload Payment Receipt**
- Click "Choose File" to select your payment receipt
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, PDF
- Make sure the receipt is clear and readable

**Step 3: Submit Registration**
- Review your information
- Click "Register"
- You'll see a confirmation message

**Step 4: Wait for Approval**
- Your enrollment will be reviewed by an administrator
- This usually takes 1-2 business days
- You'll be able to log in once approved

---

## Logging In

1. Go to the login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Login"
4. If you're not approved yet, you'll see a pending message

---

## Your Dashboard

Once logged in, you'll see your **Student Dashboard** with:

### Your Courses
- All courses you're enrolled in
- Course titles and descriptions
- Click any course to view details

### Quick Access
- Navigate back to dashboard anytime
- View your profile information
- Logout button

---

## Viewing a Course

### Course Page Shows:
1. **Course Information**
   - Course title and description
   - Overview of what you'll learn

2. **Lessons List**
   - All lessons in the course
   - Lesson numbers and titles
   - Click any lesson to start watching

3. **Course Materials**
   - PDF files related to the course
   - Download buttons for each file
   - Organized by upload date

---

## Watching Lessons

### How to Watch a Lesson:

1. **Click on a Lesson**
   - From the course page, click any lesson title
   - The lesson page will open

2. **Watch the Video**
   - The video will play automatically
   - Use YouTube controls (play, pause, volume, fullscreen)
   - Video is embedded in the page

3. **Mark as Completed**
   - After watching, click "Mark as Completed"
   - This tracks your progress
   - You can see which lessons you've finished

4. **Download Materials** (if available)
   - Scroll down to "Lesson Materials"
   - Click "Download" on any file
   - Files will open in a new tab

---

## Downloading Course Materials

### PDF Files:
1. Go to the course page
2. Scroll to "Course Materials" section
3. Find the PDF you want
4. Click "Download"
5. The file will open in a new browser tab
6. You can save it to your device from there

### Lesson Files:
1. Open a lesson
2. Scroll to "Lesson Materials" section
3. Click "Download" on any file
4. Save to your device

## Getting Help

### Need Assistance?
1. **Check this guide** - Most questions are answered here
2. **Contact your administrator** - For enrollment or access issues
3. **Technical support** - For platform-related problems

### Common Questions:

**Q: How long does approval take?**  
A: Usually 1-2 business days. Contact your administrator if it's been longer.

**Q: Can I access courses on mobile?**  
A: Yes! The platform works on phones and tablets.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**  
A: Contact your administrator to reset it.

**Q: Can I download videos?**  
A: Videos are streamed from YouTube and cannot be downloaded through the platform.

**Q: How do I know which lessons I've completed?**  
A: Completed lessons show a checkmark and "Lesson completed" message.
